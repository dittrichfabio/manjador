powershell
Remove-NetFirewallRule -DisplayName "Manjador Frontend"
Remove-NetFirewallRule -DisplayName "Manjador Backend"


And to remove the port forwarding rules:

powershell
netsh interface portproxy delete v4tov4 listenport=5173 listenaddress=0.0.0.0
netsh interface portproxy delete v4tov4 listenport=8000 listenaddress=0.0.0.0
